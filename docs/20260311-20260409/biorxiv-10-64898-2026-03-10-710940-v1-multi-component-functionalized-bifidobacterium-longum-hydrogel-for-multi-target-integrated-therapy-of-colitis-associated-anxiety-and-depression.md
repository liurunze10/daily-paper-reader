## 摘要
炎症性肠病（IBDs）常伴有焦虑和抑郁，这主要由受损的肠-脑轴信号传导驱动。然而，目前的口服疗法仍受限于肠道炎症与中枢神经系统功能障碍在空间和功能上的分离。在此，我们提出了一种基于功能化长双歧杆菌水凝胶（INPs@BL@Gel）的全方位肠-脑双区域综合治疗策略。在该策略中，黄芩苷和酪氨酸与Fe(III)配位形成无限配位聚合物（ICPs），并包覆菊粉，组装到长双歧杆菌（BL）上，随后封装在具有pH和基质金属蛋白酶响应性的丝素蛋白-明胶水凝胶中。INPs@BL@Gel表现出高载药量、有效的胃保护作用、炎症触发释放以及长期的肠道定植能力。在炎症肠道内，BL及其组分通过多靶点综合疗法协同抑制炎症反应，恢复肠道菌群稳态，并促进肠屏障修复。重要的是，BL结合相关组分显著增强了有益神经活性代谢物（如高香草酸和短链脂肪酸）的产生，这些代谢物通过肠-脑轴综合调节神经炎症、维持突触功能并促进血脑屏障修复。体内研究表明，INPs@BL@Gel不仅对结肠炎具有强大的治疗功效，并能有效缓解相关的抑郁症状，还能重塑肠道菌群并恢复屏障完整性，实现了卓越的综合治疗效果。

## Abstract
Inflammatory bowel diseases (IBDs) are frequently accompanied by anxiety and depression, largely driven by perturbed gut-brain axis signaling. However, current oral therapies remain constrained by the spatial and functional separation between intestinal inflammation and central nervous system dysfunction. Here, we present a comprehensive gut-brain dual region integrated therapeutic strategy based on functionalized Bifidobacterium longum hydrogel (INPs@BL@Gel), in which baicalin and tyrosine are coordinated with Fe(III) to form infinite coordination polymers (ICPs), coated with inulin, assembled onto Bifidobacterium longum (BL), and subsequently encapsulated within a pH- and matrix metalloproteinase-responsive silk fibroin-gelatin hydrogel. INPs@BL@Gel exhibits high drug-loading, effective gastric protection, inflammation-triggered release, and long-term intestinal colonization. Within the inflamed intestine, BL and components synergistically suppress inflammatory responses, restore gut microbiota homeostasis, and promote intestinal barrier repair through multi-target integrated therapy. Importantly, BL combined with components markedly enhances the production of beneficial neuroactive metabolites such as homovanillic acid and short-chain fatty acids, which integrated regulate neuroinflammation, preserve synaptic function, and facilitate blood-brain barrier repair via the gut-brain axis. In vivo studies demonstrate that INPs@BL@Gel not only exert potent therapeutic efficacy against colitis and effectively alleviate associated depression, but also reshape the gut microbiota and restore barrier integrity, achieving an remarkable comprehensive therapeutic effect.
