## 摘要
小胶质细胞是大脑的先天免疫细胞，能够采取与健康和疾病相关的多种激活状态。小胶质细胞激活的失调发生在多种脑部疾病中，而驱动或抑制特定状态可能具有治疗作用。为了发现小胶质细胞激活状态的调节因子，我们在诱导多能干细胞（iPSC）衍生的小胶质细胞中进行了 CRISPR 干扰筛选，以寻找六种小胶质细胞状态的抑制剂和激活剂。我们在两种不同的 iPSC 衍生小胶质细胞模型中，在单细胞转录组和细胞表面蛋白质组水平上表征了 31 个调节因子，揭示了相关状态的新蛋白标志物。我们对几个多状态调节因子进行了功能表征。ZNF532 和 PRDM1 的敲低驱动了疾病相关的、富含脂质的特征并增强了吞噬作用，同时在抗原呈递特征上表现出相反的影响。DNMT1 的敲低导致广泛的甲基化丢失，从而激活干扰素信号传导的负调节因子。这些发现提供了一个框架，用于引导小胶质细胞激活以选择性地富集小胶质细胞激活状态，定义其功能输出，并为未来的治疗提供信息。

## Abstract
Microglia, the brain's innate immune cells, can adopt a wide variety of activation states relevant to health and disease. Dysregulation of microglial activation occurs in numerous brain disorders, and driving or inhibiting specific states could be therapeutic. To discover regulators of microglia activation states, we conducted CRISPR interference screens in iPSC-derived microglia for inhibitors and activators of six microglial states. We characterized 31 regulators at the single-cell transcriptomic and cell-surface proteome level in two distinct iPSC-derived microglia models, uncovering new protein markers of relevant states. We functionally characterized several multi-state regulators. ZNF532 and PRDM1 knockdown drive disease-associated, lipid-rich signatures and enhance phagocytosis while showing opposing effects on antigen-presentation signatures. DNMT1 knockdown results in widespread loss of methylation, activating negative regulators of interferon signaling. These findings provide a framework to direct microglial activation to selectively enrich microglial activation states, define their functional outputs, and inform future therapies.
