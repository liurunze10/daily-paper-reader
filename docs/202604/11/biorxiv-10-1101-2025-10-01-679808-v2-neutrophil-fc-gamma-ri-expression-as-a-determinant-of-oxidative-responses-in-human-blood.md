## 摘要
中性粒细胞在其表面表达 Fc 受体以捕获免疫复合物。虽然 FcγRIIa 和 FcγRIIIb 在此背景下的作用已被广泛研究，但 FcγRI 的作用仍不清楚。最近，聚合 IgG 已被证明能诱导 FcγRI 的快速上调和活性氧（ROS）的产生，但这一过程的生物学相关性仍不明确。在本研究中，将健康志愿者的血液样本与热聚合 IgG（用作免疫复合物模型）一起孵育，通过流式细胞术测量，发现其迅速上调了 FcγRI 的表面表达，且主要发生在中性粒细胞上。利用聚合 IgG 刺激分离出的中性粒细胞，通过基于鲁米诺的化学发光分析监测，结果显示其以 FcγRI 依赖的方式产生了 ROS。细胞松弛素 B 增强了 FcγRI 的表达和 ROS 的产生。在静息血液中，无论是在健康志愿者还是狼疮患者中，均观察到中性粒细胞 FcγRI 与 ROS 产生之间存在正相关。本研究揭示了中性粒细胞 FcγRI 在健康个体和狼疮患者 ROS 产生中潜在的核心调节作用，并确定中性粒细胞 FcγRI 是调节氧化反应的一个极具前景的靶点。

## Abstract
Neutrophils express Fc receptors on their surface to trap immune complexes. While the roles of Fc{gamma}RIIa and Fc{gamma}RIIIb have extensively been studied in that context, that of Fc{gamma}RI remains elusive. Recently, aggregated IgGs have been shown to induce rapid Fc{gamma}RI up-regulation and reactive oxygen species (ROS) generation, but the biological relevance of this process is still unclear. In this study, incubation of blood samples from healthy volunteers with heat-aggregated IgGs, used as a model of immune complexes, rapidly up-regulated the surface expression of Fc{gamma}RI, predominantly on neutrophils, as measured by flow cytometry. Stimulation of isolated neutrophils with aggregated IgGs resulted in the production of ROS in an Fc{gamma}RI-dependent fashion, as monitored with a luminol-based chemiluminescence assay. Cytochalasin B potentiated Fc{gamma}RI expression and ROS production. In resting blood, positive correlations between neutrophil Fc{gamma}RI and ROS production were observed, both in healthy volunteers and patients with lupus. This study unveils a potentially central regulatory role for neutrophil Fc{gamma}RI in ROS production, both in healthy individuals and patients with lupus, and identifies neutrophil Fc{gamma}RI as a promising target to modulate oxidative response.
