## 摘要
背景：输注红细胞（RBCs）引起的同种免疫仍是一个显著的临床问题。然而，启动针对输注红细胞免疫反应的细胞尚未得到完全表征。最近发表的研究表明，脾脏边缘区 B（MZB）细胞对于针对红细胞产生抗红细胞同种抗体至关重要。在感染模型中，MZB 细胞的激活已被证明依赖于一类独特的边缘区巨噬细胞（MZMs）。我们假设 MZMs 会捕获储存的红细胞并将其呈递给 MZBs，最终 MZMs 是针对储存红细胞输注产生抗红细胞同种抗体所必需的。研究设计与方法：利用储存的 GFP+ 小鼠红细胞来确定脾脏巨噬细胞群的脾脏定位和红细胞吞噬作用。为了确定 MZMs 的功能影响，我们将据报道缺乏 MZMs 的 LXR-KO 小鼠与野生型小鼠进行了比较。测量了 LXR-KO 和野生型小鼠对储存的 HOD 同种异体红细胞输注的先天性和适应性免疫反应。结果：红细胞储存导致脾脏 MZMs 对输注红细胞的吞噬作用显著增加。LXR-KO 小鼠表现出 MZMs 缺失，且细胞因子 MCP-1 和 KC 的快速期产生显著降低，但 IL-6 水平相似。令人惊讶的是，抗红细胞同种抗体水平不受脾脏 MZMs 缺失的影响。结论：脾脏 MZMs 参与了对输注的储存 HOD 红细胞的先天反应，有助于 MCP-1 和 KC 细胞因子的产生。然而，MZMs 对于抗红细胞同种抗体的产生并非必需。

## Abstract
BACKGROUND: Alloimmunization to transfused red blood cells (RBCs) remains a significant clinical problem. However, the cells that initiate immune responses to transfused RBCs remain incompletely characterized. Recently published work has identified splenic marginal zone B (MZB) cells as being critically required for the production of anti-RBC alloantibodies in response to RBCs. In infectious models, MZB cell activation has been shown to depend on a unique population of marginal zone macrophages (MZMs). We hypothesized that MZMs would capture stored RBCs and present them to MZBs, and ultimately MZMs would be required for generation of anti-RBC alloantibodies in response to stored RBC transfusion. STUDY DESIGN AND METHODS: Stored GFP+ murine RBCs were utilized to determine the splenic localization and erythrophagocytosis by splenic macrophage populations. To determine the functional impact of MZMs, we compared LXR-KO mice, which have been reported to lack MZMs, with wild type mice. Both innate and adaptive immune responses to stored HOD allogenic RBC transfusion were measured in LXR-KO and wild type mice. RESULTS: RBC storage leads to a significant increase in the phagocytosis of transfused RBCs by splenic MZMs. LXR-KO mice demonstrated a lack of MZMs and had significantly decreased rapid phase production of cytokines MCP-1 and KC, but similar levels of IL-6. Surprisingly, anti-RBC alloantibody levels were unaffected by the absence of splenic MZMs. CONCLUSIONS: Splenic MZMs are involved in the innate response to transfused stored HOD RBCs, contributing to both MCP-1 and KC cytokine production. However, MZMs are dispensable for anti-RBC alloantibody production.
